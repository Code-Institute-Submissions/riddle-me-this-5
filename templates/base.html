How To Play

Welcome to the Capital Cities Quiz


To play to game follow these instructions:


1.   Select a category from the options below (10 questions per category)
2.   Enter your name before starting the quiz
3.   Go through each multiple choice question and select an answer
4.   You only get one chance per question
5.   If incorrect, the correct answer will be displayed also
6.   Your score will be shown after the last question
